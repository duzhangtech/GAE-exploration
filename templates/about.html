This web app facilitates the exchange of meal points for students at Washington University in St. Louis.

Buying Meal Points

Choose the offer that you want to buy and leave your email address; an email regarding the transaction will be sent to both you and the seller.

Selling Meal Points

Enter your amount, price per point, and contact info on the "sell" page.

Wishlist

Don't see an offer you want? Let sellers know the amount and price you are looking for by making a meal point wish.
