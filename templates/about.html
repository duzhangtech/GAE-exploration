Hello! This web application facilitates the exchange of meal points for students at Washington University in St. Louis.

Buying Meal Points

Current offers are listed on the "buy" page. Choose the number of the offer that you want to buy and leave your email address; an email regarding the transaction will be sent to both you and the seller.

Selling Meal Points

Enter your amount, price per point, and contact info on the "sell" page.

Wishlist

You can let sellers know the amount of meal points and price per point that you would agree to. If a seller sees a post they like, they can leave their email address, and an email regarding the transaction will then be sent to both you and the interested seller.
