This web app facilitates the exchange of meal points for students at Washington University in St. Louis.

Buy meal points

Click the offer that you want to buy and leave your email address. When you verify your email address, an email regarding the transaction will automatically be sent to both you and the seller.

Sell meal points

Type in your offer details. When you verify your email address, your offer will instantly appear on the market.

F.A.Q

How do I edit/remove my offer?

Type in your email here. You will get a link for directly editing/removing your offer.

What if I want to split up my meal points and sell them?

Post twice. You don't have to verify your email again.

Do I have to pay to use trademealpoints?

No. This web app is totally free to use.

Is there an Android app for this?

Yes there is.

Which meal plans can transfer meal points?

Gold, silver, bronze, and apartment.

Is it free to transfer meal points?

WashU takes a 15 point transaction fee, 7.5 points per person.
